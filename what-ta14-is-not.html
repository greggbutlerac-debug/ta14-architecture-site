Positioning

What TA-14 is not.

TA-14 is easiest to understand by drawing clear boundaries around familiar but narrower categories.

Not ordinary monitoring

Monitoring observes activity. TA-14 governs whether evidence is admissible before consequence-bearing execution.

Not audit logging after the fact

Audit logs preserve what happened. TA-14 asks whether the action should have been allowed.

Not a policy dashboard

Dashboards display controls. TA-14 governs the chain from reality to outcome.

Not only an execution gate

TA-14 includes boundary control, but begins at reality and continues through outcome.

No admissible evidence. No admissible execution.
