## Strategic purpose

The expansion pack turns the site into a public architecture portal that does three things:

1. Explains TA-14 in accessible language.
2. Protects TA-14 from being misframed as monitoring, auditing, compliance, or a simple execution gate.
3. Moves serious visitors toward paid or reciprocal review scope instead of unbounded architecture discussion.
