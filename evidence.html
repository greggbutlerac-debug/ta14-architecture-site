Evidence Governance

Information is not evidence until it is governed.

TA-14 treats evidence as infrastructure. A signal, document, output, sensor record, log, or statement must be preserved, contextualized, connected, scoped, and assessed before it can support admissibility.

Evidence continuity

Can the evidence still be traced back to the reality it claims to represent?

Evidence health

Is it complete, current, coherent, uncorrupted, and suitable for the consequence?

Evidence capacity

Is it strong enough to support reliance, binding, commit, or execution?

Evidence survivability

Does it remain admissible under pressure, latency, drift, or changing conditions?

Evidence-to-admissibility transition

At what point does evidence become strong enough to support reliance?

Evidence outrun

Has the system moved faster than its evidence can safely govern?
