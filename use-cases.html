Where TA-14 matters.

TA-14 is for systems where evidence becomes reliance, reliance becomes binding, binding becomes commit, and commit becomes consequence.

AI agent acts on stale authority

An agent has technical ability to perform an action, but its authority record has changed before commit.

HVACDR contact before evidence threshold

A refrigerant-side intervention occurs before non-invasive evidence justifies consequence-bearing contact.

Environmental sensor record without continuity

A sensor reading is used as proof even though chronology, calibration, context, or chain of custody is incomplete.

Institutional decision from incomplete records

A workflow treats records as sufficient even though the underlying evidence cannot support the consequence.

Partner architecture with a runtime gate

A tool controls execution at the boundary but lacks upstream evidence governance from reality through admissibility.

Model output treated as authority

An AI output is used as if it authorizes action rather than being evidence requiring admissibility review.
