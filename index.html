Boundaries

Public resources are proof anchors, not automatic approval.

Public links, DOI records, GitHub repositories, Google Sites, articles, Amazon records, and publication pages support chronology, authorship, preservation, public doctrine, and category clarity. They do not automatically certify, endorse, validate, approve, or production-clear any system.

Not certification of the entity

The Reviewability Check does not certify that an entity is admissible, compliant, safe, lawful, validated, endorsed, production-ready, or approved for execution.

Not architecture design

Custom route maps, evidence packet structures, implementation plans, test matrices, maturity roadmaps, or proof-object designs require paid or written scope.

Not a legal opinion

TA-14 reviews are architecture-facing and evidence-first. They are not legal representation, legal opinions, security audits, warranties, or compliance approvals unless separately agreed in writing.
