Internal review discipline

Reviewability is not admissibility.

Reviewability means TA-14 can recognize a review surface under the parent chain. The $49 Reviewability Finding must not include custom route mapping, evidence packet design, maturity roadmap, implementation guidance, or partner pathway design.
