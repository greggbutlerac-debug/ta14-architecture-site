Execution-gate specification

TA-14 Admissible Execution Gate

The gate is the decision boundary between capability and consequence. It is where an action is allowed, held, denied, escalated, or routed for review based on whether the chain is admissible enough to proceed.

ALLOW

The route satisfies the required chain conditions for its scope and risk class.

HOLD

The action may be valid, but the evidence, context, continuity, or authority is incomplete.

DENY

The route is outside scope, lacks legitimate authority, breaks continuity, or fails admissibility.

ESCALATE

The route requires human, institutional, legal, safety, or partner review before consequence.
