The commercial problem

Organizations are being asked to trust AI before the supporting evidence is ready.

Documentation can exist while authority remains unclear, evidence is incomplete, controls are untested, and execution consequences are still unresolved.

01

Claims outrun proof

Teams describe systems as safe, accurate, governed, or production ready without a current evidence package supporting those claims.

02

Controls remain disconnected

Policies, approvals, logs, tests, identities, and reports may exist separately without forming one defensible chain.

03

Execution arrives too early

The system reaches users, customers, operations, or regulated workflows before the consequence-bearing route has been made admissible.

Truthful maturity

TA-14 separates what is documented, demonstrated, implemented, and production-proven.

✓ Specifications do not automatically equal implementation.
