Founder

Greggory Don Butler

Greggory Don Butler is the founder and architect of TA-14 Admissible Execution Architecture. His work began in the field, where he identified a recurring problem: systems often move toward consequence before evidence, continuity, and authority are established.

Origin

The framework began with a field observation.

TA-14 grew from years of practical experience in HVAC diagnostics, system accountability, and evidence-based decision support. The original question was simple: how can the person relying on a system see the truth of its condition instead of being forced to trust unsupported conclusions?

“Speed without admissibility is not capability — it is exposure.”
