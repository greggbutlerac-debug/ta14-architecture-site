The Boundary

TA-14 will not sell certainty the evidence cannot support.

Every finding, recommendation, integration, and institutional pathway is limited by the materials reviewed and the written scope agreed.

Not automatic certification Review does not create regulatory approval, legal compliance certification, safety certification, or production clearance.
Not blanket endorsement A finding does not endorse an entire company, product, deployment, or unreviewed route.
Not legal or cybersecurity advice TA-14 does not replace legal counsel, penetration testing, forensic analysis, SOC review, or cybersecurity certification.

No build before boundary Private schemas, rulesets, receipts, integrations, and implementation design require written scope.
