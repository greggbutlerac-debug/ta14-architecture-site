Review Pathways

Structured evaluations for systems that cannot afford ungoverned consequence.

TA-14 engagements are designed to clarify evidence strength, admissibility gaps, execution boundaries, and readiness conditions. Reviews do not automatically certify, endorse, or validate a system.

Engagement Lanes

Choose the right review path.

AI Governance ReviewEvaluate whether AI systems have proper evidence, oversight, authority, refusal logic, and execution boundaries before action.
Execution Integrity AssessmentReview workflows where decisions create operational, institutional, financial, environmental, or human consequence.
Environmental Evidence GovernanceDevelop atmospheric evidence records, thresholds, diagnostic determination, intervention proof, and accountability pathways.
Partner / Architecture Alignment ReviewMap adjacent governance architectures against TA-14 while preserving independence and avoiding unsupported public claims.
Readiness ConversationFor early-stage teams, public-interest groups, or institutions exploring whether TA-14 applies.
